How to Export Your Data

TrainingHub can import bulk activity exports from Strava and Garmin Connect.

Strava Bulk Export

  1. Log in to Strava on a desktop browser.
  2. Go to Settings → My Account.
  3. Scroll to "Download or Delete Your Account" and click "Get Started".
  4. Under step 2, click "Request Your Archive".
  5. Strava will email you a download link (can take a few hours).
  6. Download and unzip the archive. Your activities will be in the activities folder as .fit, .gpx, or .tcx files.
  7. In TrainingHub, click Import Activity Export and select the files.

Note: You can select multiple files at once. TrainingHub supports .fit, .gpx, and .tcx formats.

Garmin Connect Bulk Export

  1. Log in to Garmin Connect on a desktop browser.
  2. Go to garmin.com/account/datamanagement.
  3. Click "Export Your Data".
  4. Select "Export Your Data" and confirm your request.
  5. Garmin will email you a download link (can take up to 48 hours).
  6. Download and unzip the archive. Activity files are typically in .fit format inside a fitness folder.
  7. In TrainingHub, click Import Activity Export and select the .fit files.

Note: Garmin's export can be large. You only need the activity .fit files — other data (wellness, sleep, etc.) is not used by TrainingHub.
